ABOUT

  • What?

    Analysis of cough sounds to distinguish a covid cough from a regular cough.

  • How?

    -data will be gathered
    -useful features will be extracted
    -features typical of a covid cough will be identified
    -model will be trained to identify these features

  • Why?

    With an exponential increase in the number of cases, 3-4 days is too long a wait to know whether one is infected or not.
